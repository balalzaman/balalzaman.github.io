The "Black Cement" 3

Originally released in 1988, the Air Jordan 3 “Black Cement” is a black, red, and gray version of Michael Jordan's third signature shoe. Designed by Tinker Hatfield, the shoe is iconic for having the signature "elephant print" around the front and back of the shoe. Coupled with rich black leather and colors that were never seen on a shoe before, this shoe was a showstopper. Back in stock for the first time since 2012, the shoe is an iconic show in the sneaker world, and is seen by many as the shoe that started the sneaker culture movement. Grab a pair for the next time you want to step out in style, on or off the court.
